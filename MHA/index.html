About us

Built by installers, not salespeople.

MediaHouseAutomation started with a simple frustration: smart home components that work brilliantly on their own but never talk to each other. So we built a team that installs everything — sound, security, lighting, access — under one standard, wired and configured to work as a single home, not a pile of apps.

Every install is handled by our own certified technicians from first site visit to final walkthrough, and we stay on call after the handover.
